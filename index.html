Agam Pandey

Applied AI Researcher

Hi! I’m Agam Pandey, a senior undergrad at IIT Roorkee. My research spans information retrieval, evals & benchmarking, and multimodal safety/robustness (VLM reasoning, adversarial ML, agents, and vision) for production AI systems.

Currently Applied AI Researcher at Mem0. Previously at Swiggy, Akki AI, EzAIx, Mindshift Analytics, and Strabag Efkon India, with published work at ICML-SCALE, NeurIPS-RML, MLRC, TMLR, and AAAI-SAPP. Off the clock, I run middle/long distance with Athletics Team IIT Roorkee and contribute to Data Science Group.
